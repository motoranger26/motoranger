About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2015, MotoRanger has grown from a small startup to one of India's leading manufacturers of premium automotive lighting and accessories. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, innovative lighting solutions that enhance both the aesthetics and functionality of vehicles.

Over the years, we've expanded our product range while maintaining our commitment to excellence. Today, we offer a comprehensive selection of automotive lighting products, from powerful off-road light bars to stylish interior lighting kits.
